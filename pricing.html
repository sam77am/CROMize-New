Billing questions

Everything About How We Charge

Is billing handled by Shopify?
+
Yes. All billing goes through Shopify's native billing system, which means it's charged to the same payment method you use for your Shopify subscription. You manage, change, or cancel your Cromize subscription directly inside your Shopify admin. We never directly charge your credit card.
What happens after the 14-day trial?
+
At the end of your 14-day free trial, Shopify will prompt you to confirm your subscription plan and begin billing. If you don't confirm, the app simply remains installed in a limited free state — we won't charge you without your explicit approval. No surprise charges.
Can I switch plans?
+
Yes, any time. Upgrading takes effect immediately and you're prorated for the remaining days in your billing cycle. Downgrading takes effect at your next billing renewal date. All plan changes are handled through your Shopify admin.
Do you offer refunds?
+
We offer prorated refunds on annual plan cancellations within the first 30 days if you're not seeing value. For monthly plans, cancellation stops future billing — we don't issue retroactive refunds for the current billing period. See our full refund policy for details.
Do you charge separately for additional features?
+
No. What you see in the plan is what you get. We don't charge per-audit, per-issue, or per-feature. Your monthly subscription covers everything listed for your tier — no hidden fees, no usage-based overages.
