We Built the Tool
We Wished Existed

Cromize started with a frustration: Shopify stores were drowning in analytics tools that showed them data without telling them what to do. We built the diagnostic layer that was missing.

The Problem We Set Out to Solve

Every Shopify store owner we talked to had the same problem. They knew they were losing sales — their analytics showed people leaving, carts being abandoned, bounce rates climbing. What they didn't know was why.

They'd install Hotjar and spend hours watching session recordings without knowing what they were looking for. They'd check Google Analytics and see numbers but no direction. They'd hire CRO consultants who delivered 40-page reports that took weeks to read and longer to implement.

The problem wasn't a lack of data. It was a lack of diagnosis. There was no tool that could look at a Shopify store and say: here's what's wrong, here's what it's costing you per month, here's how to fix it — in plain language, ranked by impact.

That's what Cromize is. The CRO doctor your store can afford, available 24/7, without a retainer.

Who We Are

Cromize is owned and operated by Kashyap LLC, a technology company based in Sheridan, Wyoming. We're a small team with deep roots in Shopify ecosystem development and conversion rate optimization.

We've worked with Shopify stores at every stage — from $5K/month DTC brands just getting traction to $2M/month operations with full growth teams. The patterns are consistent: the same types of issues appear across almost every store, and most of them are fixable without a developer, a consultant, or a months-long A/B testing programme.

Our goal is straightforward: make professional-grade CRO analysis accessible to every Shopify store, not just the ones that can afford a $5K/month agency retainer.

What We Believe

Diagnosis before prescription

You can't optimize what you haven't diagnosed. Cromize starts with a full audit — not a template, not generic best practices, but a specific analysis of your store's actual data.

Revenue impact over vanity metrics

Every issue we surface comes with an estimated monthly revenue impact. We don't rank issues by technical severity — we rank them by how much money they're costing you.

Plain English always

CRO jargon helps nobody. Every recommendation we make is written as if we're explaining it to a smart store owner who doesn't spend their days in Google Analytics.

Fixes that close the loop

Telling you what to fix is only half the job. We track whether the fix actually worked and show you the before/after numbers so you know your time was well spent.
