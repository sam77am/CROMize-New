Shipping cost reveals only at checkout — not in cart
Your $7.99 flat-rate shipping isn't shown until the customer has already entered their email on the checkout screen. This is the #1 driver of last-minute checkout abandonment. Industry data shows 48% of cart abandonment is caused by surprise costs discovered at checkout. Your cart abandonment rate (71%) is above average — this is a primary contributor.

Fix Instructions
  1. In Shopify Admin, go to Online Store → Themes → Customize your current theme
  2. Navigate to the Cart page template
  3. Look for "Shipping estimate" or "Shipping calculator" section — enable it if hidden
  4. If your theme doesn't support it natively, add the free "Estimated Shipping" app from Shopify's App Store
  5. Test the cart drawer and standalone cart page on mobile to confirm the estimate is visible before checkout
