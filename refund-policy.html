Refund & Cancellation Policy

We want you to feel completely confident using Cromize. This policy is straightforward — no tricks, no hidden clauses.

1. Free Trial

All Cromize plans include a 14-day free trial. You will not be charged anything during this period. No credit card is required to start your trial — billing is handled through Shopify's app billing system, and you'll be prompted to confirm your subscription only when your trial ends.

If you uninstall Cromize or cancel before your trial ends, you will not be charged. Period.

2. Monthly Subscriptions — Cancellation

You may cancel your monthly Cromize subscription at any time. Here's exactly what happens:

  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you retain full access to all features until then.
  • You will not be charged for the next billing period after cancellation.
  • We do not issue refunds for the current month's charge if you cancel mid-period. You've paid for the month; you keep the access for the month.

To cancel, go to your Shopify admin → Settings → Apps → Cromize → Cancel subscription. The entire process takes under 60 seconds.

3. Annual Subscriptions — Cancellation & Refunds

Annual subscriptions offer a 20% discount over monthly pricing. If you purchase an annual plan:

  • You may cancel at any time. Access continues until the end of your paid annual period.
  • If you cancel within the first 30 days of your annual subscription and have not yet received significant value from the service, you may request a prorated refund for the unused months. Contact us at info@cromize.com within 30 days of your annual billing date to request this.
  • After 30 days, annual subscription fees are non-refundable, but you retain access for the full period you've paid for.

4. Exceptional Circumstances

We handle things case by case when something unusual happens. If you were charged due to a billing error, a technical issue on our end, or a duplicate charge, we will fully refund the erroneous charge. Contact us at info@cromize.com within 14 days and we'll sort it out.

If Cromize experienced significant, sustained downtime (more than 48 consecutive hours) during your subscription period, you may be eligible for a service credit equivalent to the downtime. Contact us to discuss.

5. How Refunds Are Processed

Since billing is managed through Shopify's App Billing system, all approved refunds are processed through Shopify and appear on your Shopify account. The time it takes for a refund to appear depends on Shopify's processing time (typically 5–10 business days). We do not hold or process payment information directly — Kashyap LLC is not a payment processor.

6. No Questions Asked Cancellation

We genuinely don't make cancellation difficult. If you want to leave, we make it easy. The worst outcome is you're on a platform that isn't right for you yet — and that's fine. We'd rather you come back when you're ready than feel trapped in a subscription that isn't delivering value right now.

If you cancel and something changes — you scale up, you hire a team, your ad spend increases — you can reinstall Cromize any time and your store history will still be there.

7. Contact Us

For any billing or refund questions, email us at info@cromize.com or call +1 302 803 4764. We aim to respond within one business day.
