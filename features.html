Feature 02

AOV Opportunity Engine

We analyze the last 180 days of your order history to find products your customers already buy together — then tell you exactly how to bundle or upsell them. The recommendations come from your own data, not guesswork.

Most stores have 3–8 high-value product pairings sitting untouched in their order history. Finding them manually requires hours of spreadsheet work. Cromize surfaces them instantly.

Feature 03

Mobile Conversion Gap Detector

Mobile traffic accounts for 79% of Shopify store visits, but converts at nearly half the rate of desktop. That's not a motivation gap — it's a UX gap. We identify the specific pages and elements where mobile users are dropping off.

Unlike a heatmap that shows you clicks, we tell you what's causing the drop: a button that's too small, a form that's hard to fill on touch, a product image that loads too slowly on a 4G connection.

Feature 04

Fix Tracking & Revenue Attribution

Mark an issue fixed and Cromize checks back 14 days later. We compare your before/after conversion rate and AOV to measure whether the fix worked — and by how much. Your dashboard builds a running total of revenue recovered.

This closes the loop that most CRO tools leave open. You don't just get recommendations — you get proof that they worked.

Feature 05

Weekly Performance Digest

Every Monday morning, a short email covering the three things that matter: your current conversion rate, your AOV, and what changed this week. Not charts. Not noise. Just the signal.

New issues are flagged immediately on Growth and Agency plans. Starter users receive monthly summaries. Either way, you always know what's happening — without logging in every day.
