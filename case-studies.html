Case Study 01 — Apparel

How a Women's Activewear Brand Added $14K/Month by Fixing 3 Issues

Bloom Athletics was doing $52K/month in revenue with a solid product and good ad ROAS, but their conversion rate had plateaued at 1.3% for four months. Their founder had tried two different Shopify apps and a UX consultant without any clear direction. Cromize found three specific, fixable problems within 24 hours of install.

+27%
Conversion Rate Lift
$14K
Additional Monthly Revenue
Issue #1 — High Priority

Size guide opening a new tab

On mobile, tapping the size guide was navigating users away from the product page. 34% of visitors who opened the size guide never returned. Fix: make it open in a modal instead.

Result: Add-to-cart rate +18% on mobile
Issue #2 — Medium Priority

No return policy visible on PDP

Their 30-day free returns policy was buried in the footer. First-time buyers with activewear sizing concerns were leaving without purchasing. Fix: add a 1-line return guarantee directly under the ATC button.

Result: Checkout-started rate +11%
Issue #3 — Medium Priority

Bundle opportunity: Leggings + Sports Bra

38% of customers were already buying these together. No bundle or cross-sell existed. Fix: add a "Complete the Set" section to leggings product pages with a small bundle discount.

Result: AOV increased from $62 to $89

"I had paid a consultant $800 to look at my store and the report was basically 'improve your copy.' Cromize told me exactly which three things to fix, in what order, and why. I implemented all three in a weekend. The results showed up in the next 14-day check-in."

— Priya Mehta, Founder, Bloom Athletics
Case Study 02 — Health & Supplements

How a Supplement Brand Recovered $8,400/Month from Checkout Abandonment

Vitacore Nutrition was running strong on paid social but had a 73% cart abandonment rate — significantly above the 68% industry average. Their checkout flow had three friction points that Cromize flagged within the first audit, none of which were obvious without the data to back them up.

−31%
Checkout Abandonment
$8.4K
Monthly Revenue Recovered
Issue #1

Shipping cost reveal at checkout

$7.99 flat rate shipping wasn't visible until the checkout screen — after the customer had already entered their email. Fixed by showing estimated shipping in the cart drawer before checkout.

Abandonment dropped from 73% → 61%
Issue #2

No guest checkout option visible

The account creation screen was appearing before the guest checkout option. On mobile, users assumed they had to create an account. Fix: make guest checkout the primary option.

New customer checkout rate +22%
Issue #3

Subscribe & Save not prominent enough

Their subscription option (which increases LTV 3×) was displayed as a small radio button below the one-time purchase. Fixed by making subscription the default with an opt-down to one-time.

Subscription attach rate: 11% → 28%

"We were spending $15K a month on ads sending people to a checkout that was losing a third of them. Cromize found the leaks in the first audit. We fixed everything over two Saturdays. The difference showed up in our next payment cycle."
