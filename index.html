FAQ

Questions We Actually Get

How is Cromize different from Hotjar or just looking at my Shopify analytics?
+
Hotjar shows you what's happening — it records sessions and generates heatmaps. Your Shopify analytics shows you numbers. Neither one tells you what's actually wrong or how to fix it. Cromize is the diagnostic layer: we look at your data, identify specific issues, tell you in plain English what they're costing you per month, and give you step-by-step instructions to fix each one. It's the difference between a thermometer and a doctor.
Will installing Cromize slow down my store?
+
No. Cromize is a read-only analytics app that runs in the background. It pulls data from Shopify's APIs — it doesn't inject any JavaScript into your storefront or modify any page code. Your store speed is completely unaffected. The only time code is touched is when you choose to implement a specific fix from our recommendations.
How long until I see my first audit results?
+
Most stores get their first full audit report within 24 hours of installing. For stores with limited order history (less than 30 days of data), some features like the AOV engine will be limited initially and improve as more data accumulates. The core conversion audit runs immediately and doesn't require historical data.
I'm not technical. Can I actually implement the fixes without a developer?
+
That's exactly who we built this for. Roughly 80% of the issues we surface are fixable without any coding — things like reordering page sections, updating copy, adjusting Shopify theme settings, or adding a trust badge. Each fix comes with step-by-step instructions written for a non-technical store owner. For the 20% that do require code, we tell you that upfront so you can decide whether to hire a developer for that specific task.
What data do you access from my store?
+
We request read-only access to your orders, products, analytics, and theme data. We never store payment information, customer passwords, or any personal financial data. Our access is scoped to exactly what we need to run the audit — nothing more. You can review and revoke permissions at any time through your Shopify admin under Apps.
Can I cancel my subscription?
+
Yes, completely. You cancel through your Shopify admin (Settings → Apps → Cromize → Cancel subscription) in about 30 seconds. Billing is managed by Shopify directly — there are no contracts, no cancellation fees, and no minimum commitments. We'd obviously love to keep you, but we make leaving easy because we earn your business by being valuable, not by trapping you.
