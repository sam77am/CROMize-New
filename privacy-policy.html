Privacy Policy

At Cromize, we believe you should know exactly what data we collect, why we collect it, and what we do with it. This policy is written in plain language — not legalese — because you deserve to actually understand it.

1. Who We Are

Cromize is a Shopify application owned and operated by Kashyap LLC, a company registered in the state of Wyoming, USA (30 N Gould St, Ste R, Sheridan, WY 82801). When this policy says "Cromize," "we," "us," or "our," it refers to Kashyap LLC and the Cromize application.

If you have any privacy-related questions, you can reach us at info@cromize.com or by mail at the address above.

2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Data from Your Shopify Store

When you install Cromize, we request read-only access to the following Shopify data to provide our service:

  • Orders: Order totals, line items, product IDs, timestamps, and fulfillment status — used to calculate AOV trends and identify bundle opportunities. We do not access customer payment information or full billing details.
  • Products: Product titles, descriptions, variants, prices, and metadata — used to audit product page quality and identify upsell opportunities.
  • Analytics data: Sessions, pageviews, conversion rates, bounce rates, and device breakdown — used to identify conversion gaps and benchmark your performance.
  • Theme data: Theme structure, installed app scripts, and page templates — used to identify performance and UX issues. We do not modify your theme without your explicit instruction.
  • Store metadata: Shop name, Shopify plan, timezone, and currency — used for reporting and configuration.

2.2 Customer Data (Your Shoppers)

Cromize does not collect, store, or process the personal data of your customers (the shoppers visiting your store). Specifically, we do not access customer names, email addresses, shipping addresses, payment methods, or any personally identifiable information about your end customers. Our analysis is conducted at the aggregate level only.

2.3 Account Data

When you create a Cromize account or install the app, we collect your name, email address, Shopify store URL, and account preferences.

2.4 Usage Data

We collect standard web analytics data about how you interact with the Cromize dashboard: pages visited, features used, session duration, and browser/device type. This is used to improve the product.

2.5 Communication Data

If you contact our support team, we retain the content of those communications to provide support and improve our service.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect for these specific purposes:

  • To generate your store's conversion audit and issue recommendations
  • To calculate AOV opportunities from your order history
  • To power weekly monitoring and send your digest emails
  • To track whether fixes you implement improve your metrics
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • To process subscription billing through Shopify's billing system
  • To improve the accuracy and relevance of our audit engine
  • To send product updates and feature announcements (you can opt out any time)

We do not sell your data. We do not use your store data to train general AI models for other customers. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your store data is stored on secure servers hosted by Supabase (a SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure provider). All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption and in transit using TLS 1.3.

Access to your data is limited to Cromize application systems and the limited personnel required to provide support. We do not grant third parties access to your individual store data.

Shopify access tokens are stored encrypted and rotated according to Shopify's OAuth best practices. We do not log or expose access tokens in any external system.

5. Data Retention

We retain your store data for the duration of your active subscription, plus 30 days after cancellation to allow for reactivation. After 30 days post-cancellation, all store data associated with your account is permanently deleted from our systems.

If you request earlier deletion, contact us at info@cromize.com and we will delete your data within 7 business days.

6. Shopify-Mandated Data Handling (GDPR Webhooks)

As a Shopify App Partner, we comply with Shopify's mandatory GDPR webhook requirements:

  • Customer Data Request: If one of your customers submits a data access request, Shopify will notify us. We will respond with confirmation that we do not store that customer's personal data.
  • Customer Data Erasure: If one of your customers requests data deletion, Shopify will notify us. Since we do not store end-customer personal data, there is nothing to erase — we will confirm this within the required timeframe.
  • Shop Data Erasure: When you uninstall Cromize, Shopify sends us a shop redact webhook. We use this to schedule the deletion of all your store data from our systems within 30 days.

7. Third-Party Services

Cromize uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — database hosting (supabase.com/privacy)
  • Anthropic — AI analysis engine powering audit recommendations (anthropic.com/privacy). Data sent to the Anthropic API for analysis does not include personally identifiable customer information.
  • Resend — email delivery for digest emails (resend.com/legal/privacy-policy)
  • Sentry — error monitoring (sentry.io/privacy)
  • Shopify — app installation and billing (shopify.com/legal/privacy)

8. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on where you're located, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data.
  • Portability: Request that we export your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to our processing of your data for certain purposes.
  • Withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email info@cromize.com. We will respond within 30 days.

For California residents: we do not sell personal information as defined under CCPA. You have the right to know what data we've collected, request deletion, and opt out of any future sale (which we don't do).

9. Cookies

The Cromize marketing website (cromize.com) uses minimal cookies — strictly functional ones required for session management and login state. We do not use third-party advertising cookies. We do not use cross-site tracking cookies.

The Cromize dashboard (embedded in Shopify Admin) uses Shopify's session token system and does not rely on cookies, in compliance with Shopify's embedded app requirements.

10. Children's Privacy

Cromize is a business-to-business service intended for use by adults operating Shopify stores. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you by email (using the address associated with your Cromize account) and by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of Cromize after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
